Delicious Chicken Curry Recipe

Welcome to my cooking blog! This Chicken Curry recipe is a family favorite, blending rich spices with tender chicken. Perfect for a cozy dinner, it serves 4 and takes about 40 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (1 kg) - Boneless for quicker cooking
  • Onions (2 medium) - Finely chopped for flavor base
    • Optional: Garlic (3 cloves) - For extra zest
  • Coconut milk (400 ml can) - Adds creamy texture
  • Tomatoes (2 large) - Diced for freshness
  • Spices (2 tbsp curry powder) - Adjust to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden. Learn more about curry techniques
  2. Add spices and stir for 1 minute to release aromas.
  3. Mix in chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and tomatoes, then simmer for 25 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan bread!
